How to make a PBJ
  1. Assemble the following items
    • 2 Pieces of bread(any kind)
    • 1 jar of peanutbutter
    • 1 jar of jelly/jam
    • 1 plate
    • 1 butter knife
  2. Lay the 2 pieces of bread flat on the plate side by side.
  3. Open the peanutbutter and use the butter knife to spread the peanutbutter onto both pieces of bread
    • ** this is important to prevent soggy bread from the jelly
  4. Clean off the knife to prevent cross-contamination
  5. Open the jelly/jam and use the now clean butter knife to spread the jelly onto the bread aswell.(only on one side this time)
  6. Take the piece of bread with ONLY peanutbutter on it, and flip it over(pb side down) onto the other piece of bread(j side up)
  7. Enjoy!
